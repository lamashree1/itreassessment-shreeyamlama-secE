Our Classes

Choose from our range of yogic practices, best for your individual needs. Our gurus are the best at their field of specialization and will definitely help to bring out the best in you. The four different kinds of yogic practices are empowering and enlightening, choose what you feel your soul needs the most right now. Our class schedules are flexible, making it easier for working individuals. We have two shifts - morning shift: 8:00-11:30 am and evening shift: 4:00-7:30 pm. Sign up for what fits your routine, the benefits are the same regardless. We also conduct free workshops on weekends on topics like: food, meditation, emotions management and mental health. Our students also enjoy other perks like personalized sattvic food plan, at-home-yoga routines and meditation packages.
